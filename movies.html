Top 5 Movies

1. The Shawshank Redemption

A story of hope and friendship inside a prison. Based on Stephen King's novella, this is a timeless classic.

2. The Godfather

An epic tale of a mafia family. Power, loyalty, and betrayal play out in this unforgettable crime drama.

3. The Dark Knight

Batman battles the Joker in this action-packed masterpiece directed by Christopher Nolan.

4. Inception

A mind-bending sci-fi thriller that takes you into the world of dreams within dreams.

5. Forrest Gump

Follow Forrest through decades of life-changing events in this heartwarming and inspirational film.
